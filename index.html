ABOUT US TOUR

The About Us Tour is more than just a series of concerts—it's the story we want to share with the world. Since New Leaf formed in 2019, each song and performance has reflected our journey. It's a blend of our roots, the challenges we've overcome, and the emotions we've felt along the way. Through this tour, we invite you to experience our music in its truest form, blending British alternative rock with raw American energy. Every concert is a new chapter, a turning page in the New Leaf story.

CONNECTING WITH OUR FANS

You are at the heart of our experience.This tour isn’t just about playing live, it’s about creating a lasting connection with you, our fans, wherever you are. Whether you’ve been with us from the start or are discovering us for the first time, the About Us Tour is a way to bring us together. Every note, every lyric is an invitation into our world. We want you to feel the energy and emotion behind every performance, as we build something special together, a moment that stays with you long after the show ends.
